## ⚡ The 3 Things That Will Make or Break This

### 1. The Demo Must Be Jaw-Dropping
The first time a Daraz seller sees their bad phone photo turn into a studio image in 10 seconds — that's your conversion moment. Everything else is noise. Make this magical.

### 2. The Daraz Export Must Work Perfectly
If a seller's image gets rejected by Daraz after using SnapStudio, they churn immediately and tell everyone. The compliance engine must be tested with real Daraz listings before launch.

### 3. The Price Must Feel Like a No-Brainer
Rs. 999/month for 50 professional images vs Rs. 5,000+ per traditional photoshoot. The ROI sells itself — make sure the pricing page communicates this comparison explicitly.
